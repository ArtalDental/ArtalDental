ORTHODONTICS

AT ARTAL DENTAL WE ARE PASSIONATE ABOUT ORTHODONTICS.

It’s never too late to wear braces. And now, there are several advanced, exciting ways to straighten your teeth and finally get that beautiful smile.

Invisalign®

What is Invisalign?

 

Invisalign is a system which uses a series of clear, removable teeth aligners to straighten your teeth to achieve that perfect smile They are virtually invisible braces that are used as use as an alternative to traditional metal braces.

How does Invisalign work?

A 3-D computer-imaging technology is used to make a series of bespoke custom-made clear aligners. The individual aligners move your teeth little by little. Each aligner is worn for two weeks. By the time you are wearing your final aligner, your teeth will be in the desired final position.

How long does it take?

This will be determined by Dr. Ruiz-Mealin, as each case is individual, varies depending the complexity of the treatment you require and the amount of movement your teeth will need to make.

What are advantages of Invisalign?

* The aligners are completely transparent, and so are nearly invisible making them very popular with adults who wish to discretely undergo orthodontic treatment.

 

* Our patients who are undergoing treatment believe the aligners are more comfortable than traditional braces. This is mainly due to the aligners being removable and so eating is much easier as compared to traditional braces.

* Clinically, the aligners have less of a traumatic effect on the gums and supporting tissues. Some Invisalign treatments have been claimed to be quicker than traditional orthodontics

Will Invisalign work for me?

If you have all of your adult teeth, most patients can be treated with Invisalign, even if the teeth have crowns and bridges.

Invisalign can be used to successfully treat many issues such as the following:

  • crooked teeth,
  • spaces between the teeth,
  • certain overbites
  • certain crossbites

Is Invisalign suitable for adults?

This treatment can be used to treat anyone who has a full set of adult teeth, especially the adult molars

How long do I have to wear the retainers for?

You have to be prepared to wear the retainer at all times, other than when you are eating or drinking or brushing and flossing your teeth. This will amount to 22-23 hours per day to achieve the best results.

Will the treatment cause any discomfort?

It is normal to feel a temporary discomfort when you wear the aligners for the first few days. The aligners are smooth and so are not harmful to the inside of your mouth, but you will feel a little pressure on your teeth and this is a good sign the retainers are working.

Are there any side effects?

As with any conventional treatments, you will feel a slight discomfort at first. The aligners may also temporarily affect the speech of some people, and you may also have a slight lisp for the first few days.

How old (or new) is this technology?

This treatment was started in 1997. Since then, over 10,000,000 aligners have been made and over 480,000 patients have been successfully treated with Invisalign.  

How do I get started?

After your initial consultation, we will discuss your goals for a new smile. Photographs and impressions will be taken of your teeth, these will help us to plan your treatment with you. With our advanced computer technology, we can show you a model of how your smile will look after you have completed your treatment.

 

Lingual Braces

Lingual braces are placed behind your teeth so they are completely hidden from view. No one will know that you are wearing braces unless you tell them. These unique braces are created using state-of-the-art technology and are 100% customized to the shape of your teeth and to your dental situation.

 

 

Are the braces visible?

The braces are not visible when you are smiling.

What age is it best to have Incognito Braces?

Any age over 18 years is suitable for Incognito braces. Gone are the days when one was ‘too old’ for braces, with these latest advances in orthodontics, you are never too old!

How long does the treatment take?

Each treatment is dependent on the patient as we are all unique and so the duration will vary according to how much tooth movement is required. You will need to see Dr Ruiz-Mealin at least once a month throughout the duration of your treatment. You will start to see movement of your teeth very early on in your treatment.

 Ceramic Braces

Ceramic braces are invisible, tooth coloured braces which are so clear, no-one would know you had braces on as they are made from a translucent glass material. The braces are individually glued to the outside surface of each tooth to be moved.

Advantages of Ceramic braces are:

  • They are less visible than a metal braces
  • They allow the natural hue of your tooth to shine through
  • The brackets do not stain or discolour during the course of the treatment
  • The teeth are moved with gentle forces created by wires, elastics, and springs
  • The archwires can also be coated with a tooth coloured material to conceal their metallic appearance

 

Damon® Clear Braces

 

Advantages of Damon® Braces

  • The crystal clear braces make them virtually invisible and discreet.
  • The braces are small and have smooth contours, thus making them comfortable for the patients.
  • They move the teeth faster than traditional braces.
  • Elastic ties are not needed for the treatment
